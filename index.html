Discover the best time and place to explore India

West Bengal

Geographical Location: Eastern India, bordering Bangladesh, Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha, and the Bay of Bengal.

Best Time to Visit: October to March.

Recommended Days to Visit: 7-10 days.

Rajasthan

Geographical Location: Northwestern India, bordered by Pakistan, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, and Haryana.

Best Time to Visit: November to February.

Recommended Days to Visit: 8-12 days.

Kerala

Geographical Location: Southwestern India, along the Arabian Sea, bordered by Karnataka and Tamil Nadu.

Best Time to Visit: September to March.

Recommended Days to Visit: 6-9 days.
